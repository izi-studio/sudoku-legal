Delete Your Account

Sudoku · Last updated: 11 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Sudoku account and the data associated with it. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (instant)

  1. Open the app and make sure you are signed in (Google or Apple).
  2. Go to Settings and tap your profile card (or the Account settings menu item).
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and associated cloud data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us at izigamecompany@gmail.com from the email address linked to your Google or Apple sign-in, with the subject "Delete my Sudoku account". We will process the request within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and its link to your Google or Apple identity.
  • Cloud-synced data: game results and history, statistics, achievement progress and profile information (display name, timezone).
  • Push notification tokens and notification preferences.

What is not deleted

  • Data stored locally on your device (offline games and statistics). Uninstalling the app or clearing its data removes it.
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics that cannot identify you (see our Privacy Policy for retention details).

Playing as a guest?

Guest sessions keep gameplay data on your device. Uninstalling the app removes it. If you believe a guest session has synced data to our servers and you want it removed, contact us at the email above.
